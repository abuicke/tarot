# (VIII) Atu Name

**Key Word:** Truth  
**Song:** [song by artist](https://youtube.com/watch?v=b0cAWgTPiwM)  
**Hexagrams:** 55. 豐 Fēng / Abundance (Fullness), 42. 益 Yi / Increase (Augment), 61. 中孚 Chung Fu / Inner Truth


### Upright

1) **Guided from within**—not cast about by external circumstances.<sup>[1](#fn1)</sup>
2) **Regaining your centre**, regaining a sense of stability and self-ownership. Following your **truth**.
3) Seeing that your reactions come from inside of you, they are not forced on you by the outside world.<sup>[2,3](#fn2)</sup>



### Reversed

1) Off balance, not your normal self.
2) Focusing on external control instead of internal equanimity.
3) **Loss of centre**, disorientation due to loss of one's fundamental guiding principal.



### Card Description

Jesus walking on the water, calming the storm while the apostles are the on the boat, potentially holding a red wand or staff.
